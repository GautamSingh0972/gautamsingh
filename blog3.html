Cryptocurrency and How It Is Shaping the World

The rise of digital money and its impact on finance.

Cryptocurrency has become one of the most discussed developments in modern finance. Digital currencies such as Bitcoin and Ethereum have challenged traditional ideas about money, banking, and payment systems.

They are important because they offer new ways to transfer value, invest, and think about ownership. At the same time, they also raise questions about regulation, volatility, and long-term stability.

For students of finance, cryptocurrency is a useful example of how technology can reshape markets and create both opportunity and risk.
